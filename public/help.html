❓ Help & Guide

Strategic Honesty Content Intelligence Engine — Quick Reference

💡 Core Idea

This scheduler helps you create, approve, and publish content across all platforms from one place. Everything is anchored to your Strategic Honesty brand voice.

✍️ Content Ideas

Click Content Ideas in the nav to generate platform-specific drafts from a single core idea.

  • Enter a raw thought or insight in the Core Idea field
  • Click Adapt to All Platforms to generate drafts
  • Review Brand Alignment score — aim for Strategic range
  • Send drafts to Review & Post queue

📰 Articles

Use the Articles section to generate long-form LinkedIn articles or blog posts from your core ideas.

✅ Review & Post

All pending drafts appear here grouped by platform.

  • Click Edit to adjust tone or length
  • Click Approve to move to the Schedule queue
  • Click Post now to publish immediately

📅 Scheduling

Controls when approved posts go live. Recommended posting times (Central Time):

  • LinkedIn: Tue–Thu · 7–9am or 5–6pm
  • X: Daily · 7–9am or 8–10pm
  • Facebook: Wed & Fri · 9–11am
  • Instagram: Tue & Fri · 11am or 6pm
  • TikTok: Daily · 7–9pm

⚙️ Settings

Access Settings from the left nav to update your brand voice, posting frequency, and connected channels.
